CyberStore Android App


Project Description:
Cyber Store app is currently in its initial stages. This app is focused on concept of selling and buying books from other students. It allows direct and easy communication between students to buy and sell books to each other without the need of any third party coming in their way. The aim of this application is such that it allows students to have the ability to list books, view their books, search for books by categories, buy books, and moreover search for a specific book by name or ISBN.


Project Technologies:
  • Java
  • PHP
  • Database
  • Android Studio
